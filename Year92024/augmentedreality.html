Augmented Reality


what is Augmented Reality?

Augmented reality (AR) is a technology that overlays digital information, like images, videos, or data, onto the real world. Instead of creating a completely virtual environment like VR, AR enhances your real-world surroundings by adding digital elements to them. This is usually done through devices like smartphones, tablets, or AR glasses. For example, AR can let you see virtual objects on your kitchen table through your phone's camera or provide real-time directions while you're walking around. The aim of AR is to blend the digital and physical worlds, making your real-world experiences more interactive and informative.
