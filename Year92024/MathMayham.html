Math Mayhem

Math Mayhem focuses on improving the students ability to do qick and simple multiplication problems whilst making it fun and engagable through the use of VR headsets.

"Math Mayhem" is an immersive virtual reality (VR) game designed to make learning math fun and engaging for children. Players take on the role of a young hero in a colorful, futuristic arena filled with dynamic, sci-fi elements, solving math challenges and puzzles integrated into an adventurous storyline. Armed with a glowing math blaster, the hero navigates the environment, facing floating math problems, interactive objects, and a robotic math-monster boss guarding treasure. The game features animated numbers, a friendly robot sidekick, and a user interface that tracks progress and skill upgrades. With its vibrant visuals and engaging gameplay, "Math mayhem" combines education with excitement, ensuring a tailored and enjoyable learning experience for all players.
